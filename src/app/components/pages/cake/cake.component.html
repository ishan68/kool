The sponge is classic victoria. The Sponge as well as cream options are customizable. This sponge cake is covered on the outside with fondant / sugar icing.
Do select your ‘Allergy Restrictions’ carefully
Some cakes have icing toppers attached with cocktail sticks or other food-safe add-ons to help you accessorize the cake with toppers


All cakes should be stored in a cool dark place on a flat sturdy surface. Only Fresh cream cakes should be refrigerated.


Eggless Sponge Ingredients

Sugar, Wheat Flour (Wheat Flour, Calcium Carbonate, Iron, Niacin, Thiamin), Concentrated Whey Protein, Raising Agents: E500, E450, Whey Powder, Emulsifiers: E475, E471, E481, Cellulose Fibre, Salt, Flavourings, Stabiliser: E415, Colour: E160a(II)

Sponge with Eggs -

Wheat Flour (Wheat Flour, Calcium Carbonate, Iron, Niacin, Thiamin), Sugar, Whole Dried Egg, Raising Agents: E500, E450, Skimmed Milk Powder, Concentrated Whey Protein (MII), Emulsifiers: E475, E471, E481, Cellulose Fibre, Salt, Flavourings, Stabiliser: E415, Colour: E160a(II)


Please choose your dietary needs very carefully before placing the order to eliminate any component you are allergic to. This cake may contain wheat, gluten, eggs and soya. The cakes are made in a facility which uses ‘Nuts’ and Nuts’ byproducts
Pls Note:- All of our cakes are free from animal fat.


Please note that the cakes with icing characters may include food-safe modelling wires, spray toppers or cocktail sticks, take necessary safety measures before consumption.
